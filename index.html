About

I'm Dani Duesbury, a journalist and photographer based in Southwestern Ontario. My primary body of work is in the concert industry, having photographed over 100 concerts of various sizes and genres since my start professionally in October 2022.

I am also a recent graduate of Lambton College's Photography program in the class of 2024, during my time there I acted as the assistant lab technician during my second year. I also co-founded London based magazine "Defend Forest City" where I handled writing, interviewing bands, distributing and printing, and concert promotion. During the summer of 2024 I started work with The Sarnia Journal, covering several events for their behalf.
